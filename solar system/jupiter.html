Jupiter

Everything's bigger in Jupiter.It's the largest planet in the solar system. If you were to fit a planet the size of earth into jupiter, you could fit roughly 318 planets inside of Jupiter. That's pretty dang big! Jupiter has more moons than any other planet in the solar system.

In addition, Jupiter has the strongest gravity of any planet in the solar system. Jupiter's gravitational pull is 2.4 times greater than Earth's. So, if you've ever wanted to do some Dragon Ball Z style gravity training, just go to jupiter.
