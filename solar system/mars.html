Mars

Mars has always been an anomoly to us earthlings. In many ways, earth and mars are like cousins.
Mars and earth have roughly the same landmass. Both mars and earth have been photographed on the surface.

The first rover to ever land on mars was NASA's Mars Pathfinder Sojourner Robot, on July 6, 1997. Although NASA has sent back several from mars that the rover took, Sojourner was not able to capture any sign of life. In other words, David Bowie's question has not been answered.
