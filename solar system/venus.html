Venus

Venus is the second planet from the sun and is the hottest planet in the solar system. This is becuase Venus has a much higher concentration of carbon dioxide than other planets. This effectively traps heat into the planet.

Because of the severe heat from venus it is also the second brightest object in the night sky. Second to the moon of course.
